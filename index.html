Josh Betz

615 N Wolfe St.

Baltimore, MD 21205

I am an associate scientist in the Department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. I work in the Johns Hopkins Biostatistics Center as a statistician, data scientist, and software developer. My current collaborations include trials in amnestic mild cognitive impairment and hearing and healthy aging. I also develop software for designing, monitoring, and analyzing randomized trials.

My GitHub hosts several projects, including:

  • Tutorials on Covariate Adjustment in Randomized Trials
  • examplercts - Analysis-Ready Example Data from Randomized Trials
  • impart - Software for Flexible Study Designs and Covariate Adjustment
  • rctmle - Longitudinal TMLE for Estimating Treatment Effects in RCTs
  • picard - Planning Flexible Trials Interactively which is hosted on ShinyApps.io
